Defining Asante Rewards

As the lead designer for Asante Rewards, I developed a compelling brand identity that resonates with customers and enhances user engagement. From designing the company profile to structuring the brand’s visual elements, I ensured consistency and appeal across all materials. My work helped establish Asante Rewards as a trusted and recognizable loyalty program.

Bringing the Brand to Life - Logos, E-Shots & Marketing Assets

I created distinctive brand logos that embody the essence of Asante Rewards, ensuring versatility across different platforms. Additionally, I designed impactful e-shots and marketing materials to drive engagement and communicate the program’s benefits effectively. Every element was strategically crafted to strengthen the brand’s visibility and connection with users.

Tools, Collaboration & Impact
