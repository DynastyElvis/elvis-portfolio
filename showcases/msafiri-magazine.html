Msafiri Magazine

Msafiri Magazine Issue: 182

Msafiri Magazine, the inflight publication for Kenya Airways, continues to captivate readers with its rich content and stunning visuals. Issue 182, released in December 2023, features a diverse array of articles celebrating African culture, travel, and business.

Content Highlights

This issue delves into topics such as sustainable tourism, profiles of influential African leaders, and explorations of emerging business trends on the continent. The magazine's design seamlessly blends compelling narratives with high-quality imagery, providing readers with an immersive experience.

Design Elements

  • Utilized vibrant photography to showcase Africa's beauty and diversity.
  • Implemented clean and modern layouts to enhance readability.
  • Incorporated cultural motifs to reflect the magazine's African heritage.
